About Me

Originally from Northern Illinois, I went to college at Northwest Missouri State University with dreams of becomming a professional baseball player. When those dreams failed to materialize, I fell back on a career in the sciences, Computer Science to be exact, earning a BS-CS in 1993.

I worked for a couple years early on as a programmer, using Quick Basic, C, and Btrieve. After my initial break into the technology field, I went to work for a multibillion dollar contract manufacturer doing Visual Basic/Access DB, then shifting into web development with ColdFusion/CFML. Finally, a move to general IT in healthcare followed, however I continued developing cloud-based solutions with CFML and MS-SQL Server (db). I also sprinkled AJAX, JavaScript, and JSON in to my professional projects.
